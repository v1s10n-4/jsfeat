The Fundamental Problem

The morph blob detects EDGE DENSITY, not CARD BORDERS.

On clean paper backgrounds, edge density happens to align with card borders. On wood, wood grain edges create noise that fragments the blob or makes it overshoot. No amount of parameter tuning can overcome this — the architecture itself is the limit.
