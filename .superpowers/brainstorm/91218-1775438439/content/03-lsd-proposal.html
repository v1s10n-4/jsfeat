Why Current Approaches Hit a Ceiling

All 4 branches scored 36-37/48. Why?

They all depend on Canny edges as the foundation. Canny needs gradient magnitude — a measurable brightness difference. Dark card on dark wood has near-zero brightness difference. No matter how you process Canny edges (morph, contours, line segments, multiple channels), if Canny doesn't detect the edge, nothing downstream can find it.

LSD: Works on Gradient ORIENTATION, Not Magnitude

→ LINE SEGMENT FOUND ✓

Key Difference from Approach B (which scored 37/11)

Approach B traced Canny edge pixels into segments — still depends on Canny seeing the edge.

True LSD grows line-support regions directly from the gradient orientation field, bypassing Canny entirely. It finds lines where pixels share a consistent gradient direction, even if the gradient magnitude is below Canny's threshold. This is why LSD is called "parameter-free" — it doesn't need edge thresholds.
